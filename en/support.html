Frequently asked questions

Is the extension free?

Yes, completely free. No subscription, no ads, no "premium" tier.

Does the extension use my personal data?

No. Everything you enter is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Why do I see "Start with a search"?

The panel only runs an analysis when you are on a search-results page. On a home page or individual-listing page there is nothing to analyze.

Why does my estimate look odd?

Accuracy depends on how many comparable listings are available. The "Price my car" tab shows the number of comparables used — if it is low (e.g. under 10), treat the estimate as a rough guide. Try broadening the search (e.g. a wider year range).

Why does the crawl keep running as I navigate between pages?

The analysis pulls data from multiple results pages to improve accuracy. If you change the search criteria, a new crawl starts; as long as the criteria stay the same, progress is preserved.

Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely. Drop us a line at the address above with the idea.
