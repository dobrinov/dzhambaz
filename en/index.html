Privacy — no compromises

Авто Анализ is built around one rule: nothing ever leaves your device.

  • All computation happens locally, in your browser.
  • Nothing is sent to our servers — because there are none.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no cookies.
  • Anything you enter (mileage, year, sliders) stays only on your device.

Frequently asked questions

Is it free?

Yes, completely. No subscription, no ads, no "premium" tier.

Do I need to register?

No. Install the extension and it works immediately — no accounts, no passwords.

How accurate is the valuation?

Accuracy depends on how many comparable listings are available. The panel always shows the sample size and a confidence indicator — treat small samples as a rough guide, not an absolute number.

Does the extension use my data?

No. Everything you enter is stored locally in your browser and never leaves it. See the Privacy Policy.

Which sites does it support?

Right now — mobile.bg and cars.bg. The extension activates automatically when you open a search-results page on either site.

Which browsers does it support?

Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers.

Can I uninstall it?

Yes, anytime — from your browser's extensions menu. Uninstalling also clears all local data.
