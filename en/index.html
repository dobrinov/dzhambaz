02 — How it works

Open the listings. We do the rest.

Linear regression against comparable listings, weighted by mileage, year, and condition.

first

The extension reads the page

Once you open car search results, Джамбаз automatically collects the listings on-page — no requests to external servers.

then

It aggregates market stats

Computes median, quartiles, and distribution. You see the whole market as one number — and its shape.

finally

Suggests a realistic range

In the "Price mine" tab, enter your parameters. Linear regression and three scenarios: patient, balanced, urgent.

note

The estimates are indicative.
